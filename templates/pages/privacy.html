Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account, the application may receive information authorized through TikTok's authorization system, such as your TikTok Open ID, display name, and profile image.

2. TikTok Authorization

TikTok account access is granted through TikTok's official authorization process. The application does not ask users to provide their TikTok password directly.

3. How Information Is Used

Information received through authorized TikTok access is used to connect your account to the application and provide the application's features.

4. Token Security

Authorization credentials and tokens are stored on the application's server and are not intentionally exposed publicly.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is not shared with third parties except where necessary to provide the application's functionality or comply with applicable law.

6. Disconnecting Your Account

You may request that your connected TikTok account information be removed from the application.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the application owner through the contact information provided within the application.
